The Best Movies According to Aditi

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Drishyam

The movie is about a father who leaves no stone unturned in order to shield his family.

Howl's Moving Castle

Really cool Fantasy/Adventure movie. The movie showcases a world where people in power listen to reason, and even the villians can change for the better when shown compassion.

A Silent Voice

Super beautiful film. Really artistic. The movie depicts how a former bully is tormented and sets out to make amends.
